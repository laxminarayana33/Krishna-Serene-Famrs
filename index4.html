Krishna's Serene Farm

Heard of personalised services, but ever heard of a personalised resort? We bet not. Krishna Serene Farms is your first-of-its-kind resort which offers something for all your senses and for everyone. An exclusive private cozy retreat situated at Ponnal Village in Shamirpet Mandal, nestled amidst nature, with such close proximity to the city, it offers a raw and thrilling experience for your entire family. Unlike other resorts you can book the entire resort for yourself and soak in the goodness of luxury rooms, theme based lawns and fully equipped kitchens. Also, we have our own banana gardens from which banana leaves are used as plates. Not just that, it is also best suited for special occasions and destination weddings, clubbed with state-of-art event management solutions.
So come live it up and make memories with a range of farming exercises, indoor & outdoor activities, and non-stop entertainment.
Customize your own weekend experience, just the way you like it and we will deliver it to you in the best manner.

Covid Guidelines
Your Safety. Our Priority.

With the risks associated with COVID 19, and in keeping up with the guidelines provided by the World Health Organization and state governments we have implemented various additional measures to our resort. We have always placed the highest emphasis on the safety & wellbeing of our guests and will continue to do so.

Please find below the guidelines enlisted by the Ministry of Health and Govt. of India.

  1. Timely temperature checks are conducted on all guests and staff.
  2. Wear a mask whenever outside the room.
  3. Regular sanitisation of all rooms, common areas, kitchen and surfaces.
  4. Diligently practice food hygiene and safety measures.
  5. Wash your hands frequently with the soaps/sanitizers provided.
  6. Carry your own sanitizer and wipes for personal use.
  7. Always keep a safe distance while at the property from other guests and staff.
  8. Contact with the doorknobs are to be avoided.
  9. Contactless room service also made possible.
  10. No washing of clothes in rooms, only collected and washed as per guidelines.
  11. Anyone with symptoms of covid will immediately be directed towards medical assistance.


All our staff have been briefed on the guidelines provided by the local health authorities. The front desks are equipped with thermometers and additional measures include staff briefings regarding practicing personal hygiene, usage of masks & gloves by all, stocking up on disinfectants, as well as placing hand sanitizers at all contact areas, such as cafeterias, common areas, pool, etc.

We are doing the best we can to ensure you have a safe and fun stay!
